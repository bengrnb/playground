Gifty

Outcome

Created a prototype of a mobile application that would allow users to wish one another happy birthday and send each other gifts from anywhere in the world as well as crowdfund gift giving in order to gift more expensive items.

Client

Unsolicited tool for United States residents

Type

Product Development, UX Research, UX Design

Date

May 2023 - Current

Roles

UX Researcher, UX Designer, Product Developer

Tools

View Gifty Mockup

  • The Problem

  • It can be difficult to figure out the perfect gifts to get your friends and family. In addition to that, the gifts people often really need, are simply too expensive for a single person to pay for them.

  • The Goal

  • Create a social gift giving platform that allows for friend and family to list and receive gifts from one another. The platform would have crowdfunding features to allow of multiple people to work together to gift expensive items to the people they care about.
